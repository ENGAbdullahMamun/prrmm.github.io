Description

Worry lines, crow’s feet, frown lines and neck bands are no challenge for Botox Cosmetic. In just a few minutes, these facial paralytics can temporarily weaken facial muscles beneath the wrinkled area to relax the skin and restore its youthful appearance. The full effects of Botox® take about 10 days, but you will see the results gradually kick in throughout the week. Results last anywhere from 3-6 months depending on the individual. Before and After Your Treatment It’s not a problem for the majority of our clients, but to prevent possible bruising or swelling, take care to avoid anti-inflammatory drugs (Ibuprofen, Motrin, Advil, Aleve, Vioxx, etc.), aspirin, Vitamin E, or Ginko Biloba one week prior to treatment. Drinking alcohol prior to treatment can also increase the chances of bruising. If you are prone to bruising, you may want to pick up the homeopathic remedy, Arnica, from an area natural food store to use topically after treatment or in oral form prior to treatment.
